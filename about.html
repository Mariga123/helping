Helping Hands Organistion.

The Helping Hand Organisation was started in Dagoretti High School in 2020, after the resumption of schools after the novel coronavirus pandemic hit. Our fellow students resumed with little to nothing to school. This left many people wondering what those with even less were going through; those in poorly conditioned schools and charity dependent homes. From that, the HELPING HAND ORGANISATION was born.

Since the day of our ancestors, everyone is born in different economic backgrounds, which is a sad affair. Now with modern day capitalism, we often forget to give and elevate others because we are busy enriching ourselves. However, even in our differences, nobody should sleep hungry, even the poorest of the poorest. WE STOOD UP AND DECIDED TO CHANGE OURSELVES.


Our organisation is geared towards holistic growth of youth, both physical and mental. Our outreach involves not only material distribution but also mentorship programs and talent advancement. With its rapid growth, the group is a big happy family, with a determined team and management committee. The organisation is also young and growing.

The H.H.O team is a family that runs by principles, Christian values and chosen African morals with the basic philosophy of UBUNTU. We are always happy to see a new member and always ready to help. One is not only joining an organisation but also making lifelong friendships.

Join us today by following our journey on our social media platforms. You can also register and become a happy helper by contacting our teammate on any platform shown. We cannot wait to have you as one of us.
